What's new in ORBIT
v0.50 April 27, 2026
Send animation
Messages now launch with a satisfying flight animation when sent. It also now says who's typing and when.
v0.11 April 27, 2026
Galaxy background & themes
Added an animated galaxy canvas with shooting stars, twinkling, and Milky Way nebula bands. Five themes: Void Darkness, Inversion White, Nebula Blue, Supernova Purple, and Hydrogen Red.
v0.1 April 27, 2026
Admin panel & timeouts
Admins can now timeout members for 1m, 5m, 10m, 1h, 5h, or 10h. Timed-out users see a status message and cannot send messages until the timeout expires.
v0.0 April 26, 2026
Initial launch
ORBIT started development with real-time Firebase chat, user profiles with custom avatars, image attachments with compression, and a typing indicator.
